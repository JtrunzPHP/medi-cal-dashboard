What You Are Looking At

This platform tracks Medi-Cal managed care enrollment across all 58 California counties and maps them to publicly traded healthcare companies that participate in the Medi-Cal program. Why this matters: Enrollment changes directly impact MCO revenue. Member losses = premium revenue headwinds that hit before the market prices them in. This tool quantifies that exposure by company, by county, and over time.

Plan-to-company mapping sourced from CHCF "Medi-Cal Explained 2024" and DHCS MCP County Tables. Centene (CNC) includes Health Net + CA Health & Wellness + CalViva + CHPIV subcontracts. Molina (MOH) = direct contracts + LA County subcontract (~50% of LA Health Net members). Elevance (ELV) = Anthem Blue Cross Partnership Plan. Kaiser = private, tracked for competitive context. UNH = D-SNP only, no direct Medi-Cal contract.

Investment Signals

Tracks monthly membership trajectory for MOH, CNC, and ELV. MoM growth rate is the most sensitive leading indicator — sustained negative MoM = earnings risk. 3-month rolling momentum smooths noise for trend identification. Redetermination chart shows the full pandemic enrollment cycle: continuous enrollment surge → PHE unwinding cliff → stabilization. The guidance overlay lets you plot management's CA membership target against actual DHCS data. 6-month velocity captures medium-term momentum direction.
