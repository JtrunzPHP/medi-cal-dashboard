Methodology: Compares Dec 2023 (pre-restructuring) enrollment to latest month by ticker within each county. Shows which MCOs gained or lost members when Anthem exited many counties and the LA County contract was restructured. Positive delta = share gainer.
Geographic Concentration Risk (HHI by County)
HHI = Herfindahl-Hirschman Index — sum of squared county shares. Score 0-10,000. Higher = more concentrated in fewer counties = more policy risk. Below 1,500 = diversified. 1,500-2,500 = moderate. Above 2,500 = concentrated.
