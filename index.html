Reported vs Adjusted Methodology:
Key Adjustment — LA County Subcontract: Starting Jan 2024, Health Net (CNC) holds the prime DHCS contract in LA County but subcontracts ~50% of membership to Molina (MOH). DHCS data reports all LA County Health Net members under CNC. The "Adjusted" column reallocates 50% of LA County Health Net enrollment from CNC to MOH to reflect economic reality. This aligns with Molina's 10-K guidance of ~1.2M CA Medicaid members (vs ~600K in direct contracts only).

Sources: CHCF "Medi-Cal Explained 2024", DHCS MCP County Table, Molina 10-K (Feb 2026), Healthcare Dive reporting on Dec 2022 contract revisions.
